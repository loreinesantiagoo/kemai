About KEMAI

KEMAI PTE. LTD. We are an Singaporean company, specialising in the Direct Sourcing & Quality Assurance of Sanitary Ware Products.

We specialise in sourcing the most suitable products, at the best possible prices, while our Inspectors ensure that the sourced products are in compliance, to your required Quality Specifications.

Quality Assurance

We have a Team of Quality Inspectors that are trained and experienced, in the Inspection & Testing of Sanitary Ware.

DSQ

what we do

Direct

Kemai is your direct communicator to the factory, ensuring all inquiries, orders, and issues are communicated promptly, precisely, and correctly. We are highly experienced in the manufacture and quality requirements of Sanitary Ware. We speak your language.

Source

Kemai sources and selects the most appropriate factory to manufacture your products, then we continuously liaise with the factory and you, to ensure product dispatched schedules are monitored, advised, and met. We advise you the latest products available, or work with you to develop new products.

Quality

Kemai is your Quality Team at the factory. We inspect, test, liaise, and work with the factory, to ensure your quality requirements are fully satisfied, with reference to your country's standards and your company's specifications.
